Chilli Con Carne Recipe

Description

Chili con carne, meaning "chili with meat", is a spicy stew containing chili peppers, meat, tomatoes and optionally kidney beans. Other seasonings may include garlic, onions, and cumin. The dish originated in northern Mexico or southern Texas (Wikipedia). Follow along this BBC recipe for a delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • Vegetables
    • 1 large onion
    • 1 red pepper
    • 2 garlic cloves
    • 1 can chopped tomatoes
    • 1 can red kidney beans

  • Spices
    • 1 heaped tsp hot chilli powder
    • 1 tsp paprika
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • ½ tsp dried marjoram
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 beef stock cube
    • 2 tbsp tomato purée

  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 tbsp oil

  • To serve
    • plain long grain boiled rice
    • soured cream
    • tortilla chips
