French Onion Soup Recipe

Description

French onion soup is a type of soup usually based on meat stock and onions, and often served gratin√©ed with croutons or a larger piece of bread covered with cheese floating on top (Wikipedia). Try this easy and tasty recipe from Simply Recipes

Ingredients

  • 6 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 8 cups beef stock
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 8 slices French baguette
  • 200g grated Gruyere cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a frying pan, cook the onions in butter, stirring often, until they have softened. Add salt and sugar and continue cooking until onions are well browned
  2. Add the garlic and fry for a couple more minutes
  3. Transfer onions to a pot and add the stock, bay leaves, and thyme. Bring to a simmer, cover the pot and lower the heat to maintain a low simmer. Cook for about 30 minutes.
  4. Toast the bread on one side, then turn, add the grated cheese and toast until melted
  5. To serve, ladle soup into a bowl and transfer one cheesy toast onto the top of each bowl of soup.
